How it works

This datasource returns a timeseries showing the number of items saved to Pocket. In order to produce the timeseries, this datasource needs to connect to your Pocket account.

Click the "Connect to Pocket" button below. This will open Pocket to let you log in and authenticate the datasource.

Note: This will allow the datasource to retrieve your list of Pocket items, including the item URLs and titles. It will not allow the datasource to see your password or to modify your items.

The datasource has received an access token from Pocket. If you experience any authentication issues, you can refresh it here.

If you have not done so, create a dashboard with a panel to see your Pocket usage.
